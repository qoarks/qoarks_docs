## Introduction

Welcome to **Qoarks!** The ultimate toolkit for adding seamless authentication and authorization to your applications. Whether you're building a small app or a big enterprise system, Qoarks makes it a breeze.

<br />

## Why Choose Qoarks?

- Effortless Integration: Plug in authentication to your frontend and backend with minimal fuss.
- Fully Customizable: Adjust everything to match your brand and user experience needs.
- Top-notch Security: Keep your users safe with advanced security features.
- Easy User Management: Manage users, roles, and permissions all in one place.

<br />

Jump into Qoarks and discover how easy it is to secure your apps while having
fun! Whether you're just getting started or are a seasoned pro, you'll love how
intuitive it is to use.
